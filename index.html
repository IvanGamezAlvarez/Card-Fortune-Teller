How to play

A card will be shown; you must guess whether it is greater than, less than, or equal to the previous one. You lose instantly if you guess wrong. If you select greater than or less than and you guess correctly, you will receive 1 point. If you select equal and you guess correctly, you will receive 5. Remember that cards cannot be repeated; your score will be saved.
